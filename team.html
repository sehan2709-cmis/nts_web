👥 Meet Our Team

— The Nein to Sick Team

🚀 Our Story

The Nein to Sick team was born from a shared concern about the rising rates of depression and psychological anxiety in South Korea, particularly among university students. As a group of passionate software developers and UX/UI designers at Handong Global University, we recognized that mental health is not just a local issue but a growing global concern.

Our name combines the German word "Nein" (meaning "No") with "Sick" to represent our core mission: helping people say "no to sickness," with a special focus on mental and emotional well-being. We believe technology can be a powerful force for good when applied thoughtfully to mental health challenges.

Handong Global University

Our team is proudly based at Handong Global University, a Christian university located in Pohang, South Korea. This institution's commitment to global service and ethical innovation aligns perfectly with our mission to create technology that serves humanity's mental wellness needs. The university's international perspective and values-driven approach have shaped our vision to start locally in Korea while building for global impact.
