Introducing Bandi

Our flagship mobile application for daily emotion management, designed specifically for university students in their 20s. Bandi combines psychology-based algorithms with user-friendly design to make mental wellness accessible and engaging.

  • Simple emotion logging interface
  • Data-driven daily and weekly reports
  • Personalized comfort messages
  • Expert-written wellness content
