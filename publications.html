Our academic contributions to mental wellness technology, digital health research, and evidence-based intervention development

🎯 Research Contributions

As a research-driven team, we are committed to contributing to the academic understanding of digital mental health interventions. Our publications span multiple domains including psychology, human-computer interaction, digital health, and cross-cultural studies. Each publication builds upon our extensive research foundation of 300+ expert interviews and evidence-based development methodology.

Our work aims to bridge the gap between academic research and practical implementation, ensuring that our findings can benefit both the scholarly community and real-world mental wellness applications.
