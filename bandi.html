🌟 Key Features

Every feature in Bandi has been carefully designed based on extensive research and validated by mental health professionals

📝

Simple Emotion Logging

Quick and intuitive interface to record your daily emotions and activities, designed to take less than 2 minutes of your day.

  • One-tap emotion selection
  • Activity correlation tracking
  • Privacy-first design
  • Offline capability
📊

Data-Driven Reports

Comprehensive daily and weekly reports help you identify emotional patterns and triggers in your life.

  • Visual emotion trends
  • Pattern recognition
  • Weekly insights
  • Progress tracking
💝

Personalized Comfort

Psychology-based algorithm provides warm, encouraging messages tailored to your logged emotions and current state.

  • Evidence-based responses
  • Contextual comfort messages
  • Validated by counselors
  • Culturally sensitive
📚

Expert Content

Access to reliable articles and wellness tips written by mental health professionals specifically for university students.

  • Professional-written content
  • Age-appropriate guidance
  • Evidence-based tips
  • Regular content updates

🔬 Evidence-Based Foundation

Bandi's credibility comes from its rigorous development process and continuous validation by mental health experts

👩‍⚕️

Professional Counselors

8 licensed mental health professionals provided expertise throughout development, ensuring all features are clinically appropriate and effective for the target demographic.

🎓

Academic Validation

3 graduate students in counseling psychology contributed research insights and validated the app's psychological foundations and user experience design.

💙

User Experience Research

150 individuals with lived experience of depression and anxiety treatment provided crucial feedback to ensure the app meets real user needs and preferences.

🌍 Global Vision

Starting with Korean university students, Bandi aims to expand globally, making daily emotion management a normal part of mental self-care for young adults worldwide. Our evidence-based approach ensures cultural adaptability while maintaining clinical effectiveness.
